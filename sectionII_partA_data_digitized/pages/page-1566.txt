1. Scope 
1.1 This specification covers martensitic stainless steels 
for various structural, architectural, pressure vessel, and 
heat-resisting applications. The mechanical properties of 
these steels are customarily, but not necessarily, developed 
by a suitable heat treatment generally referred to as tem足
