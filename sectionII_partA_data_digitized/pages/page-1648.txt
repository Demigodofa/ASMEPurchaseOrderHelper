SA/EN 10222-2 ASME BPVC.II.A-2025 
SPECIFICATION FOR STEEL FORGINGS FOR 
PRESSURE PURPOSES 
Part 2: Ferritic and Martensitic Steels With Specified 
Elevated Temperature Properties 
SA/EN 10222-2 
(Identical with International Specification EN 10222-2:2017+A1:2021 with these additional requirements.) 
1. Additional Requirements 
1.1 General Requirements: 
All "see EN 10222-1" shall be read as "shall be according 
to EN 10222-1." 
1.2 Marking: 
[a) In addition to the marking requirements of this 
specification, the marking on products, and the material 
identification used on all documentation shall include: 
(1) this SA/EN specification designation 
(2) the steel grade or number 
(3) heat treatment condition as described below 
[b) Forgings shall be marked by the party performing 
the heat treatment with the letters designating the appli­
cable heat treatment condition in Table 1 of EN 10222-2 
following the stamped steel name or number. 
[c) Forgings for which normalizing has been replaced 
by normalizing forming as permitted by Note c of Table 1 
shall be marked as "+NF" instead of "+N". 
1.3 Chemical Composition: 
Note c of Table 2 shall be read as, "If only aluminum is 
used for nitrogen binding, a ratio of Al/N :::: 2 shall apply." 
l.4 Mechanical Properties: 
In Table A.2a, 1 % creep limit properties (for tempera­
tures from 380°C to 500°C), Note c for Grade X16CrMo5-1 
shall not apply. 
1.5 Resurfacing by Welding: 
If resurfacing by welding is acceptable by agreement 
with the purchaser and in accordance with para. 
6.7.2.3 of EN 10222-1 the following requirements have 
to be fulfilled: 
( a) Preparation for repair welding shall include inspec­
tion to assure complete removal of the defect. 
[b) Repairs shall be made utilizing welding procedures 
qualified in accordance with ASME Section IX and repair 
welding shall be done by welders or welding operators 
meeting the qualification requirements of ASME 
Section IX. 
l.6 Test Reports: 
1.6.1 In addition to information listed in EN 10222-1, 
para. 7, test reports shall include: 
[a) the purchaser's order number 
(b) the heat number 
1.6.2 The designation of this SA/EN specification shall 
include the edition year. 
2. National Parts 
2.1 The National Foreword and the National Annexes, if 
any, do not apply for SA/EN 10222-2. 
3. Source 
3.1 See N onmandatory Appendix A for ordering infor­
mation to obtain an English language copy of EN 10222-2 
and its references.
