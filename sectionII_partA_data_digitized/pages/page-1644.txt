SPECIFICATION FOR SEAMLESS STEEL TUBES FOR

Part 2: Technical Delivery Conditions for Non-Alloy and 
Alloy Steel Tubes With Specified Elevated

SA/EN 10216-2 
(Identical with International Specification EN 10216-2:2013+Al:2019 with these additional requirements.) 
1. Additional Requirements 
1.1 Marking: 
In addition to the marking requirements of this spec­
ification, all products required to be marked and material 
identification used on all documentation required by this 
specification are to be identified by this SA/EN specifica­
tion designation. 
1.2 Test Reports: 
The designation of this SA/EN specification shall 
include the edition year. 
2. National Parts 
2.1 The National Foreword and the National Annexes, 
if any, do not apply for SA/EN 10216-2. 
3. Source 
3.1 See Nonmandatory Appendix A for ordering infor­
mation to obtain an English language copy of EN 10216-2 
and its references.
