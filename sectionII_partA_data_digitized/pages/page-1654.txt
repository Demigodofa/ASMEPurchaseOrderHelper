SA/ISO 898-1 ASME BPVC.II.A-2025 
SPECIFICATION FOR FASTENERS MADE OF CARBON STEEL 
AND ALLOY STEEL 
Part 1: Bolts, Screws and Studs With Specified Property Classes -
Coarse Thread and Fine Pitch Thread 
SA/ISO 898-1 
(Identical with International Specification ISO 898-1:2013 with these additional requirements.) 
1. Additional Requirements 
1.1 Heat treatment: 
Fasteners shall be supplied in quenched and tempered 
heat treatment condition. 
1.2 Marking: 
In addition to the marking requirements of this SA/ISO 
specification, all products required to be marked and 
material identification used on all documentation 
required by this specification shall be identified by this 
SA/ISO specification designation. 
1.3 Certification: 
(a) Certification is required. In addition to the require­
ments of SA-962/SA-962M, the test report shall include 
results of the chemical analysis, macroetch examination, 
any mechanical tests and heat treatment method 
employed. 
(b J The designation of this SA/ISO specification shall 
include the edition year. 
2. Source 
2.1 See Nonmandatory Appendix A for ordering infor­
mation to obtain an English language copy of ISO 898-1 
and its references.
