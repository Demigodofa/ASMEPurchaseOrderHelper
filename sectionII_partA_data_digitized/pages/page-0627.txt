ASME SA-369/SA-369M replaces paras. S7 and S7.1 of ASTM A369/A369M with the following: 
S7. Minimum Cooling Rate for Grade FP91 Type 2 
S7.1 For Grade FP91 Type 2 material, the heat treatment shall ensure that following austenitizing the rate of cooling 
from 1650°F to 900°F (900°C to 480°C) is no slower than 9°F/min (5°C/ min).
