ASME SA-335/SA-335M replaces para. 13.1 of ASTM 335/A335M with the following: 
13.1 When required by 12.1.2 or 12.2 above, or when specified in the purchase order in addition to the hydrostatic test 
(12.2.3), each pipe shall be examined by a nondestructive examination method in accordance with Practice E213, Practice 
E309, or Practice E570. Except for Grades P91 Type 1 and Type 2, P92, P93, P115, P911, P122, and P128, the type of 
nondestructive examination shall be at the option of the manufacturer, unless otherwise specified in the order. Grades P91 
Type 1 and Type 2, P92, P93, P115, P911, P122, and P128 shall be examined by an examination method in accordance with 
Practice E213. When specified in the order, pipe of Grades P91 Type 1 and Type 2, P92, P93, P115, P911, P122, and P128 
shall be examined by an examination method in accordance with Practices E309 or E570, in addition to the examination 
method in accordance with Practice E213. The range of pipe sizes that may be examined by each method shall be subject to 
the limitations in the scope of the respective practices.
