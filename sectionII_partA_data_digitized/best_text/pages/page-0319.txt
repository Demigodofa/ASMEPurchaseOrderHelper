5. Heat Treatment 
5.1 Plates 1½ in. [40 mm] and under in thickness are 
normally supplied in the as-rolled condition. The plates may be 
ordered normalized, normalized and tempered, or stress re­
lieved. 
5.2 Plates over 1 ½ in. [40 mm] in thickness shall be 
normalized or normalized and tempered. 
6. Chemical Requirements

given in Table 1 unless otherwise modified in accordance with
